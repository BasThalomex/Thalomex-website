I'm a mechanical engineer with a strong belief that innovative design follows from practical experience — and from simply making things yourself. I have spent years working hands-on with conventional machines at Prodrive Technologies alongside my studies, which shaped how I approach design: grounded, manufacturable, and focused on what actually works.

I completed my MSc in Mechanical Engineering at TU/e (cum laude internships at both EPC and TNO) and I'm currently a PhD candidate at the Control Systems Technology group under Prof. Maarten Steinbuch, working on precision mechatronics for ASML-related high-tech systems. The problems I find most interesting sit at the intersection of conceptual design, precision mechanics, and real-world constraints.

Through Thalomex I make this experience available to companies that need a sharp engineer quickly — without the overhead of a large firm.
